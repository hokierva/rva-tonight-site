Why RVA Tonight

Discover everything.
Experience all of RVA.

Not another event aggregator. A utility that tells you what's happening, where, and when — no noise.

Direct from venues

Every event is sourced directly from the venue's own calendar. No outdated listings. No ghost events. If the venue says it's happening, it's in the app.

Smart calendar

See which days are packed and which are quiet at a glance. Color-coded dots show event density so you can plan your week — or your spontaneous Tuesday night.

Search everything

Find events by name, venue, category, or vibe. Quick search pills for the common stuff. Full venue directory when you want to browse. No account required.

Save & share

Heart the events you're interested in. Share them with friends via text, social, or just copy the link. Add to your Google or Apple calendar in one tap.

Dark & light mode

Follows your system preference by default, or pick your own. The dark mode is particularly nice at 11pm when you're checking what's still going on.

Fast & free

No account walls. No premium tiers. Just open the app and see what's happening, now. It loads fast because it respects your time.

How It Works

Open the app.
See what's on.

No setup. No preferences quiz. Just Richmond's events, organized simply.

Everything at a glance

The home screen shows you what's happening today and what's coming up this week. Sponsored events from local partners are clearly labeled — no hidden ads.

  • Quick stats: events this week, today, and total venues
  • Category pills to jump straight to what you care about
  • Featured events from local businesses
  • Scrollable "Coming Up" feed sorted by relevance

All the info, no fluff

Tap any event to see the full picture — date, time, venue, price, and a clean description. Then take action: buy tickets, add to your calendar, or share with friends.

  • One-tap "Get Tickets" links directly to the source
  • Add to Google Calendar or download .ics for Apple
  • Save events to your personal collection
  • Share via text, social media, email, or link
